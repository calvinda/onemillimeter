We’re a boutique solution provider serving international agencies for events in Asia.
We offer full-spectrum support from concept, design and art direction, to management and production, to technical support, logistics and consulting. Over the years, we’ve supported some of the leading global agencies on their events across Asia, providing our unique experience, networks and expertise to deliver results that work on the ground and in real time. Headquartered in Taipei, with bases in Tokyo and Shanghai, we have strong networks and experience for events in Beijing, Singapore, Bangkok and more. One Millimeter was founded in 2016 and the company has been managing projects throughout China and in Thailand, Singapore and Japan.
